GEETHANJALI INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

gist college

The GIST campus is surrounded by greenery, creating an enjoyable learning environment. It features an inspiring library, stimulating discussion rooms, large playgrounds, and state-of-the-art labs. GIST has Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with the ATAL Incubation Centre, is NBA accredited, and holds an ‘A’ grade from NAAC. It is also a UGC-autonomous institution and serves as a remote center of IIT-Bombay for the NMEICT project of MHRD, Govt. of India. Additionally, GIST is a CM’s Skill Excellence Center recognized by APSSDC and a Cyber Defence Resource Centre for National Cyber Safety and Security Standards.
